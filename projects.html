G.A.I.A – Gestural and Auditory Intelligent Assistant

Duration: 5 Months

Team Size: 1

G.A.I.A is an AI-powered desktop assistant designed to respond to both voice commands and hand gestures, enabling seamless and intuitive system interaction.

Key Features:

Voice-based system control, gesture recognition for command execution, real-time online processing.

Responsibilities:

Designed and implemented 15+ automation tasks, developed rule-based decision logic, integrated gesture and speech recognition modules, and tested system accuracy and performance.

Technologies Used:

Python, OpenCV, Pyttsx3, SpeechRecognition, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib
